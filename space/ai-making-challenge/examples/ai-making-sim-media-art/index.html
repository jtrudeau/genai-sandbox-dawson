    AI Making Challenge Simulation – Media Art Duo

    Last updated: 2025-09-21

    Project Snapshot

    • Team: Maya (Graphic Design) & Leo (Music)
    • Title: "Echoes of the Metro"
    • Project Type: Mixed-media video (AI-assisted visuals + original soundtrack)
    • Links: (placeholder)
    • SPACE Goal: Foundational Knowledge (creative tech focus)

    Iteration Log (Prompt & Validate)

    Iteration Prompt / Input AI Output Summary Validation / Checks Human Intervention
    1 Claude prompt: "Generate storyboard frames of Montreal metro at night in watercolor style" Gave moody frames but inconsistent lighting Checked for licensing & realism; noted mismatched station signage Maya re-drew key frames in Procreate, kept color palette
    2 ChatGPT prompt: "Suggest ambient sound layers for late-night metro ride" List of foley elements + minor chord progression Evaluated against field recordings; some sounds unrealistic Leo recorded actual metro sounds, composed synth pad around suggested chord progression
    3 Claude prompt: "Write narration exploring AI bias in transit design" Script leaned generic Validated against research on accessibility bias Duo rewrote sections highlighting Montreal context and personal reflections

    Artifact Build

    • Human-made: Hand-drawn frames, live-recorded audio, narration.
    • AI-assisted: Storyboard drafts, sound-layer ideas, initial script.
    • Proof: Miro board with prompt logs, Procreate layers, audio DAW screenshots.

    Ethics & Ownership

    • AI visuals heavily edited; final art credited to team.
    • Soundscape uses original field recordings; no unlicensed assets.
    • Reflection addresses bias in AI urban planning imagery.

    Reflection Highlights

    • AI sped up brainstorming but human editing defined the style.
    • Learned to question AI depictions of public spaces and representation.
    • Next step: release zine documenting process + host Discord Q&A.
