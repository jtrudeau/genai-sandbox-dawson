    SPACE Student Simulation – Nursing Persona Quickstart

    Persona Snapshot

    • Program: Nursing
    • Project Goal: Create an AI-assisted study guide that helps first-year nursing students practice medication dosage calculations safely.
    • Certificate Track: SPACE Foundational Knowledge (Science & Health focus).

    Quickstart Highlights

    1. Focus: Explore how AI can generate practice dosage problems while I verify accuracy and build confidence.
    2. Potential Risks & Ethical Considerations: AI might miscalculate dosages or omit safety warnings; mitigate by double-checking each problem with nursing formulas and faculty-approved calculators before sharing.
    3. Plan: Use Claude to draft practice questions, ChatGPT to suggest checking steps; track workflow (AI suggestion → my verification → final study card).
    4. Experiment Notes: Log prompts, highlight when AI output is wrong or missing context, note how I corrected it.
    5. Artifact: Slide deck with sample practice cards, verification notes, and tips for classmates.
    6. Reflection Prompts: What changed in my understanding? How did AI help/hinder safety? What next (perhaps integrate with simulation lab prep)?
    7. Guardrails (Humanism · Responsibility · Transparency):
    8. [ ] Transparency & Traceability: Save prompts/responses and show where AI contributed inside the study cards.
    9. [ ] Responsibility & Data Privacy: Use fictionalized patient details, store work in college-approved folders, and follow Loi 25 guidance.
    10. [ ] Equity & Integrity: Manually verify every dosage, flag uncertain outputs for mentor review, and cite AI support in final materials.
