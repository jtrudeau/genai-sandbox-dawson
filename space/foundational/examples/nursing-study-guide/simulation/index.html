    SPACE Foundational Project Template – Nursing Simulation

    Last updated: 2025-09-21

    Sandbox Snapshot

    "This SPACE foundational project lets me test AI support for nursing dosage practice while ensuring patient safety remains my responsibility."

    • [x] I can explain how AI contributed to my work.
    • [x] I respected privacy (all scenarios are fictional, no patient data).
    • [x] I credited/checked AI-generated practice questions.
    • Notes / Questions: Confirm with faculty mentor how to share deck in SPACE showcase.

    1. Project Focus

    • Exploration question or theme: Can AI help me generate safe medication dosage practice problems that align with first-year nursing competencies?
    • Who benefits: My study group of 4 nursing students; potential future cohorts via SPACE site.
    • Time window: 2025-09-15 → 2025-10-05.
    • Success looks like: A set of 6 practice cards with verified calculations and guidance on double-checking doses.

    2. Experiment Log

    Attempt Tool / Prompt What I tried What happened What I changed next
    1 Claude – "Create a dosage calc scenario for pediatric amoxicillin" Generated scenario but incorrect mg/kg Flagged error; refined prompt to include weight range and check dose with formula
    2 ChatGPT – "List verification steps for insulin sliding scale" Provided generic steps Added clinic-specific policy note and cross-checked with textbook
    3 Claude – "Draft three distractor answers for dosage math" Two distractors unrealistic Adjusted to include rounding mistakes and unit conversion traps
    • Prompts, settings, resources to remember: Include dosage formula; remind AI to show calculation steps; cross-check with drug guide.

    3. Workflow Snapshot (after refining)

    Step AI Assist My Judgment Proof / Evidence
    1 Claude drafts scenario + initial calculation I verify with dosage formula, note corrections Annotated prompt/output saved
    2 Claude suggests distractors I adjust to realistic errors, add rationale Distractor table in deck
    3 ChatGPT lists safety checks I tailor to our lab policy, add double-check steps Safety checklist slide

    4. Artifact & Story

    • Artifact format: Google Slides deck (to export as PDF for SPACE).
    • Key pieces: Scenario cards with final answers, verification notes, "How to double-check" section.
    • AI + my contribution: AI drafted scenarios; I corrected math, contextualized policies, and added reflection prompts.
    • Share plan: SPACE website submission + study group session.

    5. Reflection Prompts

    1. AI sped up idea generation, letting me focus on verifying math and teaching classmates.
    2. AI helped brainstorm scenarios but sometimes miscalculated doses—my nursing training caught mistakes.
    3. Next time I’ll incorporate an instructor-approved calculator workflow and gather peer feedback on clarity.

    6. Integrity & Verification

    Output / Decision How I checked it Confidence
    Dosage answers Recalculated manually and with drug guide High
    Safety checklist Compared with course handbook and asked TA Medium
    Distractors Confirmed they matched common mistakes High
    • Questions for SPACE mentors/coordinators: Any guidance on presenting medication examples responsibly? Can I include a short video demo?

    7. Next Steps

    • Next experiment: Pilot AI-generated simulation lab reflection prompts.
    • Skills/resources to build: Faster verification method, multimedia storytelling.
    • Feedback plan: Ask TA for review; invite study group to test cards; submit to SPACE forum.

    Prompt Boosters (Optional)

    • Starter: “Create a medication dosage scenario for adult post-op pain management that avoids patient-specific identifiers.”
    • Optimizer: “Explain the most likely mistake a student could make on this dosage problem and how to prevent it.”
