    Sandbox Spirit & Guardrails

    "SPACE projects are exploration-first while practicing the Ministry principles of Humanism, Responsibility, and Transparency. Use these guardrails to stay curious and intentional: they remind you why each checkpoint matters."

    • [ ] Transparency & Traceability: I can explain how AI contributed to every artifact and logged prompts/settings I relied on.
    • [ ] Responsibility & Data Privacy: I protected any sensitive data (mine, peers, college) and stayed within Dawson + Loi 25 guidelines.
    • [ ] Equity & Integrity: I scanned for bias or errors, cited assets correctly, and noted anything that still feels uncertain.
