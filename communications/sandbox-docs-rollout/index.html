    GENAI Sandbox Documentation Refresh – Rollout Draft

    Audience: Faculty cohort, SPACE leads, coordination touchpoints Timing: Send once review feedback returns (target early Oct 2025)

    Subject Options

    1. "Ready to explore: new GENAI Sandbox templates & quickstart"
    2. "Sandbox refresh: streamlined docs for your fall experiments"

    Message Outline

    Opening (why now) - Highlight the shift to exploration-first templates. - Mention the sandbox window (prototype runs before classroom deployment and SPACE projects through May 2026).

    What’s new - Streamlined faculty exploration template (docs/guides/faculty-sandbox-template.md). - SPACE self-directed project guide (docs/guides/space-student-guide.md). - Quickstart outline (docs/quickstart-outline.md) plus exemplar tables (workflow snapshot, verification log) and persona simulations (humanities, industrial design, physics MCQ, CS code review). - Resource hub (docs/resources.md) as the single entry point.

    How to get started - Pick an exploration goal using the quickstart. - Join the Oct 22 workshop (link to calendar invite once sent). - Log experiments with the new workflow/verification tables.

    Support & guardrails - Monthly pulse for questions; learning strategist drop-ins if needed. - Light checklist reminders for transparency, privacy, licensing. - Archive link (docs/archive/brownfield-templates-2024/) available for deeper reference.

    Call to action - Ask recipients to confirm they’ve skimmed the quickstart and templates. - Encourage sharing early artifacts for exemplars. - Provide contact for feedback (JT + PM alias).

    Draft Body Copy

    Hi all,

    The GENAI Sandbox docs are refreshed and ready for your fall experiments. We heard you: you wanted less compliance paperwork and more space to try ideas. The new exploration-first templates are live, along with a quickstart guide and examples to make documenting straightforward.

    Start here: docs/index.md (Faculty Sandbox) – links to resources, templates, quickstarts, and exemplar tables. docs/resources.md captures ongoing action items. Pick an exploration goal, drop it into the template, and capture what you learn along the way.

    What’s changed: - A lean Goal → Workflow → Evidence → Reflection → Guardrails flow for faculty projects (docs/guides/faculty-sandbox-template.md). - A matching SPACE self-directed guide for students running through May 2026 (docs/guides/space-student-guide.md). - Ready-to-use workflow and verification examples in shared exemplars and persona-specific folders under docs/examples/faculty/. - The brownfield archive lives at docs/archive/brownfield-templates-2024/ if you need the old format for reference.

    Upcoming: We’ll workshop the drafts together on Oct 22 (13:00–14:00 ET). Watch for the invite + pre-read; bring any terminology tweaks and early experiment ideas.

    Need support? Use the monthly pulse to flag questions. We’re keeping guardrails light—a quick checklist for transparency, data privacy, and licensing sits inside each template. If you’re unsure about anything, reach out to JT or reply here.

    Let’s make the sandbox a space to test, learn, and share before scaling to the classroom. Send over your first artifacts when they’re ready so we can highlight exemplars.

    Thanks! — JT & John
